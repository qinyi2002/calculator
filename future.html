What is Monte Carlo Simulation?

Monte Carlo simulation helps you plan for retirement. It allows you to visualize how you would benefit each year by knowing the maximum amount of money to withdraw from balance so that you would not have any conflict dealing with the mark uncertainties. For the stock market, we used the S&P 500 from 1926 to 2020. We obtain our AAA bond annual return and T bill from New York University's database. Monte Carlo simulation determines the success rate by randomly select a combination of ratings from our database and then calculate when will the balance exhaust. The simulation runs for 10,000 times and uses all the data gathered to determine the chance for you to have money to spend at the end of the year.
